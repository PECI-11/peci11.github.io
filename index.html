About the project

Context

As we all know, tourism is one of the main economic activities in the country and technologies have become essential for experiencing the success or failure of tourism in a destination.

In order to evolve tourism, the DMOs had as a solution the implementation of Destination Management Systems, that are online collaborative networks, which interconnect all the relevant tourist agents of a destination, facilitating the communication and cooperation between them. However, most of these were unsuccessful.

Project Proposal

Proposal

The intention is to implement a platform that supports the multiple services and provides them to multiple DMOs in the perspective of the front end, constituting the websites of Destination Management Organizations the customers of these services, organized by region or by type of service.

Goals

  • Create a single back-end and API that is capable of supporting multiple DMSs.
  • Develop examples of websites from different DMOs with the back-end and the API.
  • Easy and fast interaction with any organization and adaptation to any front end.
  • Interactive as possible to allow any DMO to be able to use it and take advantage of its potential.

Methodology

In order to obtain the expected result, a single front end was created for stakeholders.

The stakeholders can:

  1. Register their companies;
  2. Submit the services they have so it can be available in multiple websites in the future;

These services are mapped and saved according to their geographic data and the type of service.
DMOs who wish to use this platform will have their own front end with information about their region. This front end will be powered by the back end of the platform, through requests made to the API.
