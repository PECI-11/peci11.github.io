About the project

The principal goal of this project is to implement a fast and easy-to-use platform for associated organizations to provide services for various Destination Management Systems.

To obtain the expected result, a single front end was built for stakeholders, providing them with a way to register their companies and the services they can provide. These services are mapped and saved according to their geographic data and the type of service.​ OGDs that want to take advantage of this platform will have their own front end with information about their region. This front end will be powered by the back end of the platform, through requests made to the API.
